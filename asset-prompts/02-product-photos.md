# Product Photography Requirements

> These assets require REAL product photography — not AI-generated.
> They show actual LumiBox products that customers will receive.

---

## Photography Style Guide

### Overall Aesthetic
- **Background**: Clean white or soft cream (#F5F1E8)
- **Lighting**: Soft, diffused natural light (or studio equivalent)
- **Mood**: Warm, inviting, premium but approachable
- **Style**: Scandinavian minimalism meets playful children's products

### Technical Specifications
| Type | Dimensions | Format | Background |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| Box packaging | 800x800px | WebP | Pure white or cream |
| Flat-lay items | 1200x800px | WebP | White/cream with subtle shadows |

### Props & Styling
- Minimal props — let products speak
- Optional: Small plant sprig, wooden elements, soft fabric
- Avoid cluttered compositions
- Consistent styling across all shots

## Delivery Checklist

### Before Photoshoot
- [ ] All physical products ready and assembled
- [ ] Branding/labels applied to boxes
- [ ] Lighting setup matches Year 1 photos
- [ ] Background paper/surface prepared

### During Photoshoot
- [ ] Capture multiple angles (select best later)
- [ ] Ensure consistent lighting across all shots
- [ ] Check focus and sharpness
- [ ] Verify colors appear accurate

### Post-Production
- [ ] Color correct to match existing assets
- [ ] Crop to specified dimensions
- [ ] Export as WebP (quality 85-90)
- [ ] Name files exactly as specified
- [ ] Place in `assets/images/lumibox/` directory
