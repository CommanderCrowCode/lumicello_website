Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What age range are the Exploration Kits designed for?

Our First Year Collection is designed for babies 0-12 months, with six age-specific kits tailored to each developmental stage. We're working on expanding to toddler and preschool age ranges soon!

How does the Curiosity Fingerprint™ work?

The Curiosity Fingerprint™ is our unique system that creates a multidimensional profile of your child's interests, learning styles, and engagement patterns. It evolves over time to provide increasingly personalized recommendations.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we ship within Thailand. We're planning to expand to other Southeast Asian countries soon. Sign up for our newsletter to be notified when we launch in your area!

Can I cancel or modify my order?

Yes! You can cancel or modify your order within 24 hours of placing it. After that, please contact us as soon as possible and we'll do our best to accommodate your request.

Is the digital platform available yet?

We're currently in development and will be launching soon. Join our community to get early access and be the first to know when we launch!
